The Game Changer: Study Partners

Here's the thing - studying with the right person can literally change your life. We were perfectly complementary - where I had intuition, she had perseverance; where she excelled at perfectionism, I brought different perspectives.

The Magic Formula

Here's what actually worked for me:

  • Always attend lectures - Catch up before each lesson
  • Talk to people - Friends, professors, anyone who can help
  • Coffee is life ‚òï (seriously)
  • During exam season: Repetition, mind maps, sticky notes everywhere
  • Create comprehensive study documents - I wrote detailed guides for each subject

This might sound crazy, but hear me out - book your vacation during exam season. Whether the session goes well or badly, having those plane tickets ready is way better than crying and scrolling Instagram. Trust me on this one! üèñÔ∏è

There's something magical about having that escape plan ready. It keeps you motivated during the tough study sessions and gives you something to look forward to. Plus, if things go well, you can celebrate properly. If they don't... well, you have a beach waiting for you to recover!

Study Materials & Resources

Throughout my degree, I created detailed study guides that helped me (and many classmates) succeed. The one I'm most proud of is my Computer Networks guide - it's comprehensive and has helped tons of students. You can find it and other materials linked in my CV section.

These documents weren't just notes - they were complete knowledge bases that I could rely on during exams. Writing them forced me to really understand the material, and sharing them helped build a study community.

Final Thoughts

University is hard, there's no sugar-coating it. But with the right approach, the right people, and maybe a little bit of luck in finding that perfect study partner, it can also be the best time of your life. The friendships you make, the knowledge you gain, and the person you become - it's all worth it.
