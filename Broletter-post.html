I Built a Free AI Newsletter That Learns What I Like

10 March 2026 Projects
AI Automation Open Source Telegram Personal

What is Broletter?

Broletter is a free, self-adapting daily science newsletter powered by Gemini, arXiv, and Telegram. It learns from your feedback and delivers personalized articles every day, at zero cost.

How It Works

  • AI-Powered Selection: Uses Gemini to scan arXiv and select papers based on your interests.
  • Feedback Loop: Your likes/dislikes on Telegram are used to improve future recommendations.
  • Automation: Runs daily, fully automated, with no manual curation required.
  • Open Source: Built with transparency and extensibility in mind.

Why I Built It

I wanted a newsletter that adapts to my interests, costs nothing, and leverages modern AI tools. Broletter is my solution—combining open-source code, cloud automation, and feedback-driven learning.

Tech Stack

  • Google Gemini API for AI recommendations
  • arXiv for science content
  • Telegram Bot for delivery and feedback
  • Python, serverless scripts, and GitHub Actions

Get Started

Try Broletter or check out the source code on GitHub.

Broletter is free, open, and adapts to you. Feedback and contributions are welcome!
